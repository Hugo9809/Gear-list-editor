# Code of Conduct

We are committed to creating an inclusive, safe, and respectful community.

Expected behavior
- Be respectful and considerate of others.
- Communicate constructively; avoid personal attacks and harassment.
- Be mindful of cultural differences and diverse perspectives.
- When you disagree, seek common ground and explain your viewpoint with empathy.

Prohibited behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation based on race, gender, sexuality, disability, religion, or any other characteristic.
- Offensive language, threats, or disruptive behavior.
- Dasking or belittling others for asking questions or offering feedback.

Reporting and handling
- If you experience or witness misconduct, please report it to the project maintainers via the Issues page or direct contact (as appropriate).
- All reports will be handled with discretion and seriousness.
- We may take action including warnings, temporary removal from contributions, or bans as necessary.

Scope
- This Code of Conduct applies to all project communications, including issues, pull requests, chat rooms, and direct messages related to the project.

Thanks for helping us build a welcoming project community.
